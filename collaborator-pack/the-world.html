06 · The First IP

06 · 第一个 IP

Ritual & Ruin. Ritual & Ruin。

Tiny creatures hauling blood through forbidden rituals, evolving into something wrong, then hunting each other in their final moments. This is the world the collective is building first.

细小的生物在禁忌仪式中搬运血液，进化成某种不该存在的东西，然后在它们最后的时刻互相猎杀。这是这个共创团体首先要建造的世界。

What It Is它是什么

Ritual & Ruin is a 2v2 local co-op party game for PC. Two teams of small creatures race to carry blood to ritual altars. The altar performs forbidden experiments on the creatures that serve it — granting power, demanding form. Power that accumulates. Power that transforms cute into something that shouldn't exist. When the transformation completes, the hunt begins: the evolved creature becomes something with a proximity aura that kills. Sudden death. One team outlasts the other. The experiment resets.

A match takes five to ten minutes. It is designed to be played on a couch with people you know, with a shared screen, in a room where you can hear each other react.

《Ritual & Ruin》是一款 PC 端 2v2 本地合作派对游戏。两支由细小生物组成的队伍竞相把血液搬运到仪式祭坛。祭坛对那些为它服务的生物进行禁忌实验——赐予力量，索取形态。力量不断积累。力量将可爱的形态变成某种不该存在的东西。变化完成后，猎杀开始：进化完毕的生物拥有了一个会杀死靠近者的感应光环。突然死亡。一队熬过另一队。实验重置。

一局游戏耗时五到十分钟。它被设计成坐在沙发上、和你认识的人一起、共用一块屏幕来玩的——在一个你们可以互相听见彼此反应的房间里。

You work together to carry blood to altars, and the ritual grants you power. Power that transforms cute into something that shouldn't exist. Then the hunt begins. 2v2 local PC party game. 5–10 minutes per match. Can you outlast?" The Aesthetic Arc美学弧线

The game starts cute. Small, round creatures. Soft edges. The colour palette is pastel violet and near-white with lavender — something between a children's toy and a dark fairy tale. The creatures have the visual grammar of something safe.

Then they start changing.

The ritual altar does something to the creature closest to it. Each completed ritual is a step along an arc: base form → empowered form → terminal form. With each stage, the softness cracks. The edges distort. Something underneath the cute shape starts to show through. By the time the terminal form arrives, what's in front of you is unambiguously a cosmic horror — something that should not exist, wearing the ghost of what it used to be. The cute was never fake; it was the starting point of a transformation. That's what makes the end state land.

This is the core visual language: dark kawaii evolving into cosmic horror. Not a costume change — a becoming. The horror is earned. It comes out of the cute, and it carries the cute inside it the whole way through.

游戏开始时是可爱的。小巧、圆润的生物。柔软的边缘。调色板是粉彩紫和近白色加薰衣草——某种介于儿童玩具和黑暗童话之间的东西。这些生物具有某种"安全"的视觉语法。

然后它们开始改变。

仪式祭坛对距它最近的生物做了某件事。每次仪式完成，都是沿着一条弧线走出的一步：基础形态 → 强化形态 → 终末形态。随着每个阶段的推进，那种柔软开始破裂。边缘开始扭曲。可爱形状之下的某种东西开始透出来。等到终末形态降临时，站在你面前的东西毫无疑问已经是一种宇宙恐怖——某种不该存在的东西，披着它曾经所是之物的鬼影。那种可爱从来不是假的；它是一段变化的起点。这才是终态如此有力量的原因。

这就是核心视觉语言：黑暗川普进化成宇宙恐怖。不是换了一套戏服——而是一个成为的过程。那种恐怖是被挣来的。它从可爱中长出来，并且一路携带着那份可爱。

The Theme主题

The game is about persistence in the face of uncertainty.

How far do you keep going when things get tough — when you don't know if it will pay off, when the system you're in is degrading around you, when there's no external reason to continue other than your own will to carry on? The game doesn't ask you to win. It asks how long you'll outlast. And when you finally get there, the payoff isn't what you expected. The machine just stops. Life goes on. You did it. That's it.

The end screen reflects this exactly. Clinical mode: cold experiment log. "Team A outlasted Team B by X.XXX seconds. Experiment reset in 10 seconds." No celebration. Only measurement. The system that ran you through all of that does not care that you made it. It is already preparing for the next round.

This is not nihilism — it's the opposite. The point is that you persisted anyway. That the continuation was the thing, not the reward. The machine's indifference is what makes the decision to keep going meaningful.

这个游戏关于在不确定中坚持。

当事情变得艰难时，你会走多远——当你不知道坚持是否值得，当你所在的系统正在周围你崩解，当除了你自己继续下去的意志之外没有任何外部理由？游戏不要求你赢。它问你能熬多久。而当你终于到达那里，那份回报并不是你期待的。机器只是停了。生活继续。你做到了。就这样。

结算画面精确地反映了这一点。冷静模式：冷酷的实验日志。"A 队比 B 队多坚持了 X.XXX 秒。实验将在 10 秒后重置。"没有庆祝。只有测量。那个把你推过这一切的系统，并不在意你撑过来了。它已经在准备下一轮了。

这不是虚无主义——恰恰相反。重点在于你无论如何都坚持下去了。坚持本身才是那件事，不是那个奖励。机器的漠然，正是让"继续下去的决定"变得有意义的东西。

The sub-theme: power breaks your form副主题：力量打碎你的形态 Tapping into something bigger than yourself grants capability — and costs you your form. The ritual empowers the creature closest to the altar. Power accumulates. The body can't hold it. Too much, and you become the terminal form: something that shouldn't exist, hunting the thing it used to be. The aesthetic arc (cute → cosmic horror) is the sub-theme made visible. External power does not elevate you cleanly. It transforms you, and it takes the original shape with it as it goes. 接入某种比你自身更宏大的东西，能赋予你能力——也会夺走你的形态。仪式赋予距祭坛最近的生物力量。力量积累。身体无法承载它。太多了，你就成为终末形态：某种不该存在的东西，猎杀它曾经所是之物。美学弧线（可爱 → 宇宙恐怖）正是副主题的视觉化。外部力量不会干净地提升你。它改变你，并且在改变的过程中带走了原本的形状。

The World Behind the Game游戏背后的世界

The game's fiction is deliberately thin at the surface. You play as engineered creatures — small, purposeful, designed for a specific function: to carry blood to altars, to be close to power, to endure. Two teams. Two conditions in an experiment. Not moral factions; test groups. A/B conditions in a study whose designers have long since stopped watching.

The civilisation that built the altars is ancient and absent. They created systems designed to run without them. The altars still run. The creatures still run. Whatever the experiment was testing, the results have been irrelevant for longer than the creatures' entire existence. The system continues because systems continue. That's what systems do.

This is intentional negative space. The world doesn't explain itself at you. You're inside a machine that has been running longer than you've existed, and you will never know what it was for. What you know is that you are here, that there is blood to carry, that there is an altar that wants it. That's enough to act.

游戏的虚构在表面上刻意保持稀薄。你扮演被设计出来的生物——细小、有目的、为特定功能而生：搬运血液到祭坛，靠近力量，忍耐。两支队伍。一场实验里的两种条件。不是道德阵营；是检验组。一项研究的 A/B 条件——而这项研究的设计者已经不知道何时开始不再观察了。

建造了祭坛的那个文明是古老的、缺席的。他们创造了被设计成无需他们即可运行的系统。祭坛依然运行着。生物依然运行着。无论这场实验在检验什么，那些结果与这些生物的整个存在历史等长的时间里已经不再有意义了。系统继续，因为系统就是会继续。这就是系统的运作方式。

这是刻意的负空间。这个世界不会向你解释自己。你身处一台运行时间远超你存在之前的机器里，你永远不会知道它是为了什么。你知道的是：你在这里，有血液要搬运，有一个想要它的祭坛。这已经足够行动了。

Where It's At Right Now现在在哪里

The prototype is playable. Blood physics are in — ObiFluid simulation, physical slosh and spill. The relay mechanic works. The altar filling mechanic works. The evolution system and terminal phase exist in early form. The floor art has its direction: near-white violet-tint tiles, neon lavender grout with glow emission, Voronoi crack degradation, pink-magenta glow. The creature visuals are placeholder — jellyfish-like, readable, functional, not yet the actual art direction.

What doesn't exist yet: the visual identity of the creatures across all three forms. The sonic world — no committed audio direction yet. The visual language of the world beyond the floor tile. The specific way the world feels from inside it, expressed across all the mediums that will eventually carry it.

That's exactly the work a collective does together. The prototype is a proof of concept for the mechanic. The world — the thing the IP actually is — is still being built. That's why the invitation is open now, not later.

原型已经可以玩了。血液物理已经实装——ObiFluid 模拟，真实的晃动与泼洒。传递机制有效。祭坛填充机制有效。进化系统和终末阶段以早期形态存在。地板美术已有方向：近白色带紫色调的地砖，霓虹薰衣草灌缝带有发光效果，Voronoi 裂纹降级，粉洋红色光晕。生物视觉是占位符——类水母形态，可读性强、功能性强，但尚不是真正的美术方向。

目前还不存在的东西：三种形态下生物各自的视觉身份。声音世界——目前还没有确定的音频方向。超越地砖本身的世界视觉语言。这个世界从内部感受起来的那种特定质感，需要通过最终将承载它的所有媒介来表达。

那正是共创团体一起去做的工作。原型是机制层面的概念验证。这个世界——IP 真正是的那个东西——仍在被建造。这就是邀请现在就开放、而不是之后才开放的原因。

What this means for your medium这对你的媒介意味着什么 The world has a theme, a mechanic, a visual direction for one surface, and a starting aesthetic. What it doesn't have is a complete sonic identity, a fully developed visual language for the creatures across three evolutionary stages, a story grammar, or a sense of what the world carries outside the game itself. Each of those is an open question that belongs to the collective — not to be answered by one person and executed by everyone else, but worked out together, with each medium genuinely shaping what the others become. 这个世界有主题、有机制、有一个表面的视觉方向、有一个起始美学。它没有的是：一个完整的声音身份，一套完整发展的、覆盖三个进化阶段的生物视觉语言，一套叙事语法，以及一种"这个世界在游戏本身之外承载着什么"的感觉。这些每一个都是开放的问题，属于这个共创团体——不是由一个人回答、然后由所有人执行，而是一起把它们梳理出来，每一种媒介都真正地塑造其他媒介所变成的东西。
