06 · The First IP

06 · 第一个 IP

Ritual & Ruin. Ritual & Ruin。

Tiny creatures hauling blood through forbidden rituals, evolving into something wrong, then hunting each other in their final moments. This is what we have right now, not a finished world, but a seed. The theme and the mechanic are established. Everything else (the creatures, the world, the fiction) is open to be shaped by the people who build alongside it. Your medium doesn't adapt the game. It stands next to it, and together they define what this world actually is.

细小的生物在禁忌仪式中搬运血液，进化成某种不该存在的东西，然后在它们最后的时刻互相猎杀。这是我们现在拥有的，不是一个完成的世界，而是一颗种子。主题和机制已经确立。其余的一切（生物、世界、虚构）都开放给与之并肩建造的人去塑造。你的媒介不会去改编游戏。它站在游戏旁边，两者一起定义这个世界真正是什么。

What The Game Is游戏是什么

Ritual & Ruin is a 2v2 local co-op party game for PC. Two teams of small creatures race to carry blood to ritual altars. The altar performs forbidden experiments on the creatures that serve it, granting power, demanding form. Power that accumulates. Power that transforms cute into something that shouldn't exist. When the transformation completes, the hunt begins: the evolved creature becomes something with a proximity aura that kills. Sudden death. One team outlasts the other. The experiment resets.

A match takes five to ten minutes. It is designed to be played on a couch with people you know, with a shared screen, in a room where you can hear each other react.

《Ritual & Ruin》是一款 PC 端 2v2 本地合作派对游戏。两支由细小生物组成的队伍竞相把血液搬运到仪式祭坛。祭坛对那些为它服务的生物进行禁忌实验，赐予力量，索取形态。力量不断积累。力量将可爱的形态变成某种不该存在的东西。变化完成后，猎杀开始：进化完毕的生物拥有了一个会杀死靠近者的感应光环。突然死亡。一队熬过另一队。实验重置。

一局游戏耗时五到十分钟。它被设计成坐在沙发上、和你认识的人一起、共用一块屏幕来玩的，在一个你们可以互相听见彼此反应的房间里。

You work together to carry blood to altars, and the ritual grants you power. Power that transforms cute into something that shouldn't exist. Then the hunt begins. 2v2 local PC party game. 5–10 minutes per match. Can you outlast?" The Aesthetic Arc美学弧线

The game starts cute. Small, round creatures. Soft edges. The creatures have the visual grammar of something safe.

Then they start changing. Each completed ritual is a step along an arc:

  1. Base form. Soft, round. The visual grammar of something safe.

  2. Empowered form. The softness cracks. The edges distort. Something underneath the cute shape starts to show through.

  3. Terminal form. Unambiguously cosmic horror, something that should not exist, wearing the ghost of what it used to be.

This is the core visual language: dark kawaii evolving into cosmic horror. Not a costume change. A becoming. The horror is earned. It comes out of the cute, and it carries the cute inside it the whole way through. The cute was never fake; it was the starting point of a transformation. External power does not elevate you cleanly. It transforms you, and it takes the original shape with it as it goes.

游戏开始时是可爱的。小巧、圆润的生物。柔软的边缘。这些生物具有某种"安全"的视觉语法。

然后它们开始改变。每次仪式完成，都是沿着一条弧线走出的一步：

  1. 基础形态。柔软、圆润。某种"安全"的视觉语法。

  2. 强化形态。柔软开始破裂。边缘扭曲。可爱形状之下的某种东西开始透出来。

  3. 终末形态。毫无疑问已经是宇宙恐怖，某种不该存在的东西，披着它曾经所是之物的鬼影。

这就是核心视觉语言：黑暗川普进化成宇宙恐怖。不是换了一套戏服。是一个成为的过程。那种恐怖是被挣来的。它从可爱中长出来，并且一路携带着那份可爱。可爱从来不是假的；它是一段变化的起点。外部力量不会干净地提升你。它改变你，并且在改变的过程中带走了原本的形状。

The Theme主题

The game has two loops. The surface is a party game: small creatures, blood, altars, five-minute rounds. Fun, chaotic, couch-ready. Each match runs, resets, runs again. That loop works on its own terms, and players who only ever stay there get a complete experience.

The real game is the inter-match layer. What accumulates across rounds. What changes between sessions. The system logging results, running on, generating experiment numbers, and slowly, ambiguously, starting to break.

The breakdown doesn't start immediately. It starts exactly when the player shifts from figuring out the game to wanting to get good at it. The moment they lean forward is when the floor starts being pulled away. Things start feeling slightly wrong. Rules that seemed stable develop noise. The little game becomes less fun, less playable, in a way that's hard to locate. And crucially: the UI makes clear this isn't a bug. The interactive elements outside the game (the log entries, the system readouts, the experiment numbers) start signalling that something is happening. The system is breaking from beyond the game itself.

Players who keep going aren't doing it because it's rewarding. They're doing it because there might be an ending, and they want to see it. But they're not sure. That uncertainty is the point. That is the test.

Along the way, players can do things the system wasn't designed to handle, including unexpected inputs and actions that introduce noise into a machine already accumulating errors. Maybe those actions accelerate the breaking. Maybe they do nothing. The causality is ambiguous by design. Did I do that? lives simultaneously with or was that already coming? Neither resolves. Both are accurate to how complex systems actually fail: not because someone pulled the right lever, but because accumulated noise pushed past an invisible threshold. No single action caused the collapse. All of them did. None of them did.

The ending answers nothing. The whole thing ends: the little game, the creatures inside it, the creatures that managed to escape, the creatures that will never be born because the system broke before they could be. Life goes on, elsewhere, indifferently. That's it.

After the story ends, the player gets back a clean, fully functional version of the little game. No breaking. No degradation. The game is the same. But the player is different. They're playing with creatures they know are mortal, in a system they've watched break, with full knowledge of what was lost. Joy with full knowledge, the rarest emotional destination in any medium.

This is not nihilism. The point is that you persisted through genuine uncertainty, through a game that stopped being fun, through questions that were never going to be answered, and got to the end anyway. That continuation was the thing, not the reward.

游戏有两条循环。表面是一款派对游戏：小生物、血、祭坛、五分钟一局。有趣、混乱、适合沙发上玩。每一局开始、重置、再开始。那条循环本身就成立，只停留在那里的玩家也得到一份完整的体验。

真正的游戏在局与局之间的那一层。跨越多局所累积的东西。在多次游玩之间所发生的变化。这个系统记录结果、持续运行、生成实验编号，然后缓慢地、含糊地，开始破裂。

破裂不是立即开始的。它恰好在玩家从"搞懂这游戏"转向"想把它玩好"那一刻开始。他们身体前倾的时刻，就是地板开始被抽走的时刻。事情开始感觉稍微不对。看起来稳定的规则发展出杂音。这个小游戏变得不那么有趣、不那么可玩，以一种很难定位的方式。关键是：UI 明确表明这不是 bug。游戏之外的交互元素（日志条目、系统读数、实验编号）开始发出信号，表明某件事正在发生。系统正在从游戏自身之外破裂。

继续下去的玩家不是因为它有回报。他们这么做是因为可能有一个结尾，他们想看到它。但他们不确定。那种不确定就是重点。那就是测试本身。

一路上，玩家可以做系统并未被设计来处理的事情，包括出乎意料的输入和向已在累积错误的机器中引入杂音的行动。也许那些行动加速了破裂。也许它们什么也没做。因果性在设计上就是含糊的。是我做的吗？与还是它本来就要发生？同时存在。两者都不被解决。两者都准确地反映了复杂系统实际如何崩溃：不是因为某个人扳动了正确的拉杆，而是因为累积的杂音越过了一个不可见的阈值。没有任何单一行动导致了崩溃。所有行动都导致了。没有行动导致了。

结尾没有回答任何东西。整个事情就这样结束了：这个小游戏、它里面的生物、设法逃脱的生物、永远不会被诞生的生物，因为系统在它们能够被诞生之前就破裂了。生命在别处、漠然地继续。就这样。

故事结束之后，玩家会得到一份干净、完全可运作的小游戏。没有破裂。没有降级。游戏是一样的。但玩家不同了。他们在和自己知道是会死的生物玩，在一个他们见过破裂的系统里玩，带着对失去之物的完整认识。带着完整认识的喜悦，任何媒介中最罕见的情感目的地。

这不是虚无主义。重点是你穿过了真正的不确定，穿过了一个不再有趣的游戏，穿过了永远不会被回答的问题，并且依然走到了终点。那种继续下去本身就是那件事，不是奖励。

The Story Underneath底下的故事

The creatures weren't chosen. They were engineered, small living interfaces, built to endure proximity to power, to carry blood to altars, to serve a function. Not heroes. Not worshippers. Infrastructure.

The civilisation that built the altars is ancient and gone. They created systems designed to run without them. The altars still run. The creatures still run. The experiment has been producing results for longer than the creatures' entire existence, results no one is reading, for a question no one remembers asking. The system continues because systems continue.

The 2v2 framing is the system's own description of its experiment, its marketing copy for itself. The teams are not moral factions. They are A/B conditions. Two test groups in a study whose designers stopped watching a very long time ago. The system logs cross-team cooperation as competitive parameters consistent with expected behaviour. It was never built to see what actually happened between the specimens.

But the experiment isn't testing the creatures. It's testing the player. How long will they continue operating the experiment after it stops being fun? The logs aren't documenting creature survival times. They're documenting persistence: how many rounds before the player stops, what they try when the system starts to feel wrong, how long they push toward an ending they can't confirm exists.

The difficulty doesn't teach mastery. It escalates demands. What the system asked at the start is less than what it asks by the end. That was always true. The game just takes a while to reveal it.

The world doesn't explain itself. You're inside a machine that has been running longer than you've existed, and you will never know what it was for. What you know is that you are here, that there is blood to carry, that there is an altar that wants it. That's enough to act.

生物不是被选中的。它们是被设计出来的，小型的活体接口，被建造来承受靠近力量的状态，搬运血液到祭坛，履行一项功能。不是英雄。不是崇拜者。是基础设施。

建造祭坛的文明是古老的、已经消失的。他们创造了被设计成无需他们即可运行的系统。祭坛依然运行。生物依然运行。实验产出结果的时间已经比这些生物的全部存在还要长，那些结果没有人在读，针对的是一个没有人记得在问的问题。系统继续，因为系统就是会继续。

"2v2"这个框架是系统对自己实验的自我描述，它给自己写的营销文案。两支队伍不是道德阵营。它们是 A/B 条件。一项研究里的两个测试组，而这项研究的设计者很久很久以前就不再观察了。系统把跨队合作记录为"符合预期行为的竞争参数"。它从未被建造来看清楚标本之间实际发生了什么。

但实验不是在测试生物。它是在测试玩家。在它不再有趣之后，他们还会继续操作这个实验多久？日志记录的不是生物的存活时间。它们记录的是坚持：玩家在多少局之后停止、当系统开始感觉不对时他们尝试什么、他们朝着一个无法确认是否存在的结尾推进了多久。

难度并不教授精通。它只是不断升级要求。系统一开始要求的，少于它最后要求的。这一直是真的。游戏只是花了一段时间才把这件事揭示出来。

这个世界不解释自己。你身处一台运行时间远超你存在之前的机器里，你永远不会知道它是为了什么。你所知道的是：你在这里，有血液要搬运，有一个想要它的祭坛。这已经足够行动了。

This is a framework, not a fixed canon这是一个框架，不是固定的正典 The creatures, the civilization, the altars, the fiction, these are a starting shape, not a locked brief. Your medium may need different answers to the same questions. What do the creatures look like in your world? What does the absent civilization mean through your lens? Those aren't gaps to fill. They're invitations to shape what this world actually becomes. The only load-bearing elements are the theme and the mechanic. Everything else is open. 生物、文明、祭坛、虚构，这些是一个起始形状，不是被锁定的简报。你的媒介对同样的问题可能需要不同的答案。在你的世界里这些生物长什么样？通过你的镜头看，缺席的文明意味着什么？那些不是要填补的空缺。它们是邀请，邀请你塑造这个世界真正会变成什么。唯一承重的元素是主题和机制。其他一切都是开放的。

The Emotional Arc情感弧线

The feel of the world follows a progression, not just within a single match, but across the full experience.

  1. Fun. Silly party game energy. Chaos and laughs. Couch-ready.

  2. Something's off. Glitches begin. Behaviours drift. The game feels slightly wrong in a way that's hard to name.

  3. Not fun anymore. The system has degraded enough that playing becomes harder, stranger. The party-game feel is slipping.

  4. Push through anyway. The game is broken. Players continue, not because it's rewarding, but because something has to come out the other side.

  5. It just ends. No fanfare. No victory. The system stops. Life goes on.

The overarching constraint is erosion. There is no single moment where fun becomes not-fun, or where the system flips from running to broken. Not a mode switch. A gradient. The demoralization is authored. The frame is allowed to mourn the player.

这个世界的感受遵循一条递进的弧线，不只是在单局之内，而是贯穿整个体验。

  1. 有趣。傻气的派对游戏氛围。混乱与欢笑。适合沙发上玩。

  2. 有点不对劲。小故障开始出现。行为开始漂移。游戏感觉稍微不对，以一种很难命名的方式。

  3. 不再有趣。系统已经退化到玩起来变得更难、更奇怪的程度。派对游戏的感觉正在滑落。

  4. 无论如何继续推进。游戏已经破裂。玩家继续，不是因为它有回报，而是因为某种东西必须在另一端出现。

  5. 它就这样结束。没有庆祝。没有胜利。系统停止。生命继续。

总体的约束是侵蚀。没有任何一个单一时刻让"有趣"变成"不有趣"，或让系统从"运行"翻转到"破裂"。不是模式切换。是一个渐变。这种士气崩溃是被作者写出来的。框架被允许为玩家哀悼。

Where It's At Right Now现在在哪里

The prototype is playable.

What's built:

  • Blood physics: fluid simulation, physical slosh and spill
  • Relay mechanic
  • Altar filling mechanic
  • Evolution system and terminal phase as systems
  • Placeholder creature visuals (jellyfish-like, readable, functional, not yet the actual art direction)

What's open:

  • The visual identity of the creatures across all three forms
  • The sonic world (no committed audio direction yet)
  • The visual language of the world beyond the floor tile
  • The colour palette (no committed direction yet)
  • The specific way the world feels from inside it, expressed across all the mediums that will eventually carry it

That's exactly the work a collective does together. The prototype is a proof of concept for the mechanic. The world, the thing the IP actually is, is still being built. That's why the invitation is open now, not later.

那正是共创团体一起去做的工作。原型是机制层面的概念验证。这个世界，IP 真正是的那个东西，仍在被建造。这就是邀请现在就开放、而不是之后才开放的原因。

What this means for your medium这对你的媒介意味着什么 The game establishes the theme and the core mechanic. What it doesn't, and can't, establish is what this world is in your medium. The creature designs, the visual language, the sonic identity, the fiction's texture, the story grammar: none of that is decided. Not because it hasn't been done yet, but because those answers belong to the people building alongside it. 游戏确立了主题和核心机制。它没有、也无法确立的，是这个世界在你的媒介里是什么。生物的设计、视觉语言、声音身份、虚构的质地、叙事语法：这些都没有被决定。不是因为还没有人去做，而是因为那些答案属于那些与之并肩建造的人。

This isn't "here's the game, now make a comic version of it." Your medium doesn't adapt what the game already is. It stands next to it as its own work, shaped by your creative instincts, your aesthetic, your understanding of what this world carries. The conversation between your medium and the game is what produces the IP. Neither is upstream of the other.

这不是"这是游戏，现在做一个漫画版"。你的媒介不会去改编游戏已经是的样子。它作为它自己的作品站在游戏旁边，由你的创作直觉、你的美学、你对这个世界承载着什么的理解所塑造。你的媒介与游戏之间的对话，才是产生 IP 的东西。两者中没有谁在另一方的上游。

And that conversation goes both ways. What your medium needs from this world, what it surfaces, what it asks, what it makes possible, is welcome to change the game itself. A composer asking "what does persistence sound like mechanically" might reshape a system. A visual artist's answer to what these creatures are might redefine how they feel to play. That's not scope creep. That's the work.

而且那场对话是双向的。你的媒介从这个世界需要什么、它揭示什么、它问什么、它使什么成为可能，都被欢迎改变游戏本身。一位作曲家问"坚持在机制上听起来是什么样"可能会重塑一个系统。一位视觉艺术家对"这些生物是什么"的回答可能会重新定义它们玩起来的感觉。那不是范围蔓延。那就是工作本身。
