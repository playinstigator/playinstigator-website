Ritual & Ruin

2v2 co-op chaos. Carry blood. Evolve. Hunt.

Tiny creatures carry blood to forbidden altars. The altar empowers whoever stands closest — at a cost. Evolve into something terrifying. Then the hunt begins.

Ritual & Ruin is a 2v2 local co-op competitive party game for PC. Five to ten minutes of chaos, coordination, and sudden betrayal. For friend groups that have exhausted everything else.

Format 2v2 / Local Co-op
Platform PC — Steam
Session 5–10 minutes
Status Alpha Prototype
