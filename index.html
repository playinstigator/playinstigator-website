Ritual & Ruin

Can you outlast?

Tiny creatures hauling blood through forbidden rituals, evolving into something wrong, then hunting each other in their final moments. You work together to carry blood to altars, and the ritual grants you power. Power that transforms cute into something that shouldn't exist. Then the hunt begins.

2v2 local co-op competitive party game for PC. 5–10 minutes per match. For friend groups that have exhausted everything else.
