a 90-second walkthrough

Turn your track into a playable level.

This is Game Dev Beats — the little rhythm game we throw on to break the ice at our co-working sessions. 2–6 of us tap along to a track, and whoever does the worst answers the icebreaker question first. Here's the ask: we'd love the music to come from you. Send us one track and it becomes a level — your song, played to a room full of game devs.

how it plays
①
Your song
One finished track — the music players hear. mp3 / wav / m4a.
②
A beat sheet
The exact moments players should tap. You'll make it here by tapping along to your own song.
So here's what we need: one finished track (keep it under 2 min) plus a beat sheet you'll make right here — then just DM me both. One song each, first come first served (only a handful of slots). Takes about 90 seconds.
