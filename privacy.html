Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Who we are

Play Instigator is an indie game studio based in Singapore, currently developing Ritual & Ruin. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages at playinstigator.com.

What we collect

The only personal data we collect is your email address, and only if you voluntarily subscribe to our mailing list via the sign-up form on this site.

We do not collect names, payment details, location data, or any other personal information through this website.

Why we collect it

We use your email address solely to send you updates about Ritual & Ruin and Play Instigator — development progress, playtesting invitations, and release announcements. We will never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

How your data is processed

Our mailing list is managed by Kit (formerly ConvertKit), a US-based email service provider. When you subscribe, your email address is stored and processed by Kit on our behalf.

Kit's privacy policy is available at kit.com/privacy.

Cookies

This website itself does not set cookies. However, the Kit sign-up form embedded on this site may set cookies to function properly. GitHub Pages may also collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type) as part of standard web hosting — see GitHub's privacy statement.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Unsubscribe from our mailing list at any time using the link in any email we send
  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data from our mailing list

These rights apply under Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) and, for EU/EEA residents, under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your data is removed from our active mailing list. Kit may retain anonymised analytics data — refer to their privacy policy for details.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, email us at playinstigator@gmail.com.
